Project Overview

The Daily Finance Tracker is a robust full-stack solution designed to empower users with comprehensive control over their personal finances. By bridging the gap between raw data and visual analytics, this application simplifies the complexity of income management and expense auditing.

Key features include real-time balance calculations, categorical data segregation, and dynamic graphical reporting to ensure users make informed financial decisions.
